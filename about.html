About

This project is inspired by the Delta Zeta creed and explores themes of growth, self-reflection, and faith. Each page represents a different part of that journey, using color, typography, and interaction to express emotion and change over time.

The pink slides represent stillness, introspection, and softness, while the green slides symbolize growth, nature, and movement. Elements like flowers, leaves, and expanding text visually communicate transformation and personal development.

Through this website, I aim to create a calm but meaningful experience that reflects how growth happens gradually and how faith ties everything together.
